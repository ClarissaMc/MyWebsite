Hey, my name's Clarissa (she/her) and I'm a full-stack developer from Weymouth, MA. I'll be graduating from the University of Massachusetts Boston in May 2024 with a BS in Computer Science and am excited to apply my skills to the real world!

Being part of such a fast, constantly-evolving field requires an immense desire to learn and improve. This is a large reason why I love computer science as much as I do. It presents me with countless opportunities to expand my knowledge and understanding while also being able to physically apply what I learn. This spring, I took two online, certified courses through edx for cloud computing and web development. Over the summer, I taught myself Python and made four different projects that showcase the wide range of applications of the language. Currently, I'm working on a machine-learning project that predicts the length of a trip on the MBTA (If you, too, have had to suffer through the unpredictability of the T, you have my condolences).

My recent projects have focused on web development and data science, but I've also recently taken an interest in machine learning.
